# Multi-Sheet Workspaces

Sheets let you maintain multiple independent visualizations in a single session, all connected to the same data source.

---

## Adding sheets

Click the **+** button in the sheet tab bar at the bottom (or top, depending on your layout) to add a new sheet.  
Each sheet starts blank — drag fields to build an independent chart.

---

## Switching between sheets

Click any sheet tab to switch to it. Sheets are independent: changing fields, filters, or properties on one sheet does not affect the others (except [Global Filters](../filtering/global-filters.md), which are shared).

---

## Renaming sheets

Double-click a sheet tab to enter edit mode, type the new name, and press **Enter**.

---

## Duplicating a sheet

Right-click a sheet tab and choose **Duplicate**. The new sheet starts as an identical copy — useful as a starting point for a variation of an existing chart.

---

## Reordering sheets

Drag sheet tabs left or right to reorder them.

---

## Closing sheets

Click the **×** on a sheet tab to close it. You will be prompted to confirm if the sheet has unsaved changes.

---

## Undo / Redo per sheet

Each sheet maintains its own independent undo/redo history. Undo on Sheet 2 will not affect the state of Sheet 1.

| Action | macOS | Windows / Linux |
|---|---|---|
| Undo | `Cmd+Z` | `Ctrl+Z` |
| Redo | `Cmd+Shift+Z` | `Ctrl+Y` |
